About Drake

Drake is one of the best-selling artists in the world, known for his blend of R&B, rap, and pop music. He is widely considered one of the most successful music artists of the streaming era.

Drake, Whos Birth name is Aubrey Drake Graham, (born October 24, 1986, Toronto, Ontario, Canada). He has many Aliases, some such as "The 6 God", "Champagne Papi", or commonly known as "Drizzy".

OVO Sound

Drake founded his own record label "OVO Sound" with help of Noah “40” Shebib and Oliver El-Khatib in 2012. Notable Artists under this Label are Drake, PARTYNEXTDOOR, and Smiley.
